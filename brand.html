05 — Motion
/* Survival bars */
 transition: width 1.2s cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94);
Triggered by IntersectionObserver at threshold: 0.3
/* Accordions (FAQ, ingredients) */
 max-height: 0 → max-height: 400px;
transition: max-height 0.3s ease;
/* Video ticker */
 animation: ticker-scroll 28s linear infinite;
Pause on hover: animation-play-state: paused;
/* CTA buttons */
 transition: opacity 0.15s, transform 0.15s;
Hover: opacity 0.85 · Active: transform scale(0.98)
✗ Never: transition-all
/* General rule */
 Only animate transform and opacity.
Every interactive element needs: hover + focus-visible + active states.
06 — Copy Voice
Tone
  • → Clinical, direct-response, data-driven
  • → Removes personal guilt — blames the environment
  • → Mechanism before claim
  • → Specificity over superlatives
  • → Respects the reader's intelligence
Required verbatim phrases
"desk bloat" "cubicle gut" "3 PM crash" "gastric mortality" "dead bacteria" "post-lunch brain fog" "fishy burps" "the acid furnace"
Key data points — always use these exact numbers
Survival: powder 0.003% / standard capsule 2.8% / MAKTREK® 50%+
Delivery increase: 1,685% more live bacteria than unshielded
CFU: 40B on label → 20B+ at destination
Refund: 30-day money-back · no return required
Subscription: none — one-time purchase only
07 — Do / Don't
✓ Do
"Your stomach acid destroys 97–99.997% of standard probiotics before they reach your lower intestine."
"40B CFU on the label. Over 20B CFU at the destination."
"This isn't a discipline problem. It's a mechanics problem."
"The variable isn't you. The variable is the chair."
✗ Don't
"Feel your best every day!"
"Support your gut health naturally."
"Start your wellness journey today!"
"Our innovative formula helps you thrive."
