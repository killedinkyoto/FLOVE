# Tina — Avatar Profile

## Demographics
- **Age:** 25–45
- **Gender:** Female
- **Location:** Major corporate/metropolitan business environments
- **Income:** High-earning professional/executive salary tier
- **Roles:** Management consultants, corporate attorneys, financial analysts, software architects

## Psychological Profile
Hyper-competitive corporate high-achiever. Takes immense pride in cognitive stamina, decision-making speed, and executing deliverables under pressure. Silently experiences extreme physical vulnerability. Views her body as a machine to be fueled with caffeine and pushed to its limits.

**Core tension:** She excels professionally but her body is failing her — and she blames herself.

- Low gut alpha diversity triggered by screen time and physical immobilization

### 2. Workplace Digestive Humiliation & High-Stakes Anxiety
- Panic over sudden, uncontrollable stomach gurgling in quiet conference rooms or depositions
- Abdominal cramping striking mid-presentation or during executive board dinners
- Forced to make excuses and leave meetings early due to physical distress

### 3. Corporate Dependency Loop & Supplement Failure
- Expensive health routines (green juices, meal kits, supplement stacks) sit unused due to unpredictable hours
- Heavy caffeine loop depletes Vitamin B1 → severe afternoon cognitive crashes
- Trapped in "subscription traps" or suffering gas, bloating, and "fishy burps" from unshielded competitor supplements
