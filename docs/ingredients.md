## MAKTREK® Bi-Pass Technology — How It Works
1. **Primary Casing:** Probiotic strains, prebiotic fibers, and Marine Polysaccharide Complex encapsulated in high-grade hypromellose (vegetable) capsule
2. **Secondary Gel Shield:** Upon entering the stomach, gastric juices dissolve the outer vegetable capsule. Moisture immediately contacts the Marine Polysaccharide Complex. The complex hydrates instantly, expanding into a secondary, internal protective gel capsule around the living probiotic cells.
3. **Acid-Resistant Matrix:** Marine-derived polysaccharide matrix is highly resistant to low-pH environments. Remains intact through stomach acid. Bacteria fully insulated from chemical destruction.
4. **Neutral-pH Release:** Once the protected matrix enters the duodenum (neutral-to-alkaline pH 6.0–7.5), the marine polysaccharide shield naturally disintegrates. Fully active, highly viable probiotic strains released directly into the lower intestinal tract to colonize and support the gut barrier.
