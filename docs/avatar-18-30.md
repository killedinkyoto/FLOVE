# Flove 18–30 Avatar Sheet

## Demographic
- Age: 18–30, 100% Female
- Location: English-speaking digital fitness communities (r/xxfitness, r/ibs, gym/retail spaces)
- Income: Moderate-to-high disposable. Spends on high-compression activewear, structured fitness programs, personal coaches, $99/month greens powders
- Identity: The disciplined "Fit Chick" — macro-tracker, dedicated gym-goer, performance culture enthusiast

## Pain Point 1: The Activewear Paradox & "Imposter Bloat"
- Sudden, erratic abdominal distension that pushes lower stomach out until she looks "four to six months pregnant"
- Abdominal expansion that drowns out hard-earned muscle definition even in a strict caloric deficit
- High-compression leggings, sports bras, and crop tops transform from confidence-boosting to instruments of physical restriction and psychological distress

## Pain Point 2: The Gut-Stripping Fitness Trap
- Zero-calorie artificial sweeteners (sucralose, aspartame) in pre-workouts and energy drinks → gut dysbiosis, pathogen overgrowth, painful gas
- Sugar alcohols (erythritol, xylitol) in "low-carb" protein bars → osmotic water draw into colon → fermentation → immediate distension
- Whey protein concentrate → high residual lactose → overwhelms digestive enzymes → severe "protein bloat"

## Pain Point 3: The Solution Value Gap
- Superfood greens powders → mid-lift abdominal cramping from raw insoluble prebiotic fibers (inulin/chicory root) fermenting under training pressure
- Standard OTC probiotics → dissolve in stomach acid (<1–2.8% survival) → burning acid reflux → zero results
- Laxative and detox teas → abdominal spasms, bathroom emergencies, dehydration, bowel dependency

## Short-Term Goals
- Eliminate post-meal and post-workout abdominal distension to maintain a flat stomach all day
- Confidently wear high-compression gym sets and tight evening outfits without "sucking in" or hiding under oversized t-shirts
- Capture weekly coaching check-in / progress photos that display true muscular definition without water/gas interference

## Long-Term Aspirations
- Rebuild a resilient, balanced gut microbiome capable of optimizing high-protein athletic nutrition without systemic inflammation
- Break free from cycles of extreme fasting, caloric restriction, and toxic detox regimens used to fight bloating
- Establish a predictable, stress-free aesthetic reality where physical appearance aligns with military-grade discipline and work ethic
